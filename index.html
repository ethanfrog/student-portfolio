About Me

I am graduate of the University of Michigan-Flint, and I am seeking my first full-time position in the field of web development. I majored in computer science and minored in graphic design, and I enjoy web development because it takes both of these fields into account. My other interests include app development, information technology, typography and digital artwork.

I joined a web development boot camp at MSU in order to bolster my skill set. Although I explored a good amount of programming languages during college, I only touched on the basics of most of them. As I want to make a career out of web design, I felt that I should have a deeper understanding of HTML, CSS, JavaScript and everything else that goes into designing high-quality websites.
